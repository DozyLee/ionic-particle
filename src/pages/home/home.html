Welcome to myPillow!

Why myPillow?

  • myPillow is a pillow that vibrates at a time you set on the "myPillow" app.
  • myPillow aids those with hearing disabilities since the device utilizes vibrations instead of sound.
  • myPillow is also another option for people who don't like the classic sound alarms!
How to Set Up Alarms
  1. Open up the "myPillow" app
  2. Tap "Create Alarm"
  3. Set desired alarm time and press the check-mark button
  4. And you're done!
    5. Once you create an alarm, on the upper-left hand corner, click the parallel lines to open up a menu. You can edit or delete alarms by clicking on "Alarms".
About the Creator

A random human being who participates in this TACC camp. She thought a vibrating pillow alarm was cool and thought about how she couldn't wake up to any sort of sound alarms. The idea began to flourish and soon "myPillow" was born.
